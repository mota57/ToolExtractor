Sanghi Polyesters Limited is a Public incorporated on 03 January 1986. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Vijayawada. Its authorized share capital is Rs. 1,000,499,970 and its paid up capital is Rs. 1,000,000,000. It is inolved in Spinning, weaving and finishing of textiles.

Sanghi Polyesters Limited's Annual General Meeting (AGM) was last held on 28 June 2008 and as per records from Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), its balance sheet was last filed on 31 December 2007.
