Balaji Agro Oils Limited is a Public incorporated on 02 May 1994. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Vijayawada. Its authorized share capital is Rs. 140,000,000 and its paid up capital is Rs. 105,770,952. It is inolved in Production, processing and preservation of meat, fish, fruit vegetables, oils and fats.

Balaji Agro Oils Limited's Annual General Meeting (AGM) was last held on 30 September 2021 and as per records from Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), its balance sheet was last filed on 31 March 2021.

Directors of Balaji Agro Oils Limited are Balaji Vallabhaneni, Nagendra Babu Koduru, Ramesh Babu Gottipatti, Koteswara Rao Kodali, Venkatramaiah Vallabhaneni, .
